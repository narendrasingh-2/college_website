About Shri Bapuji Group of Education

Established with the vision of imparting value-based education, Shri Bapuji Group of Education is dedicated to the intellectual and spiritual growth of students.

Mission: To provide quality education and instill strong moral values.Vision: To become a center of excellence in professional and value education.

Our Philosophy: We believe in "Education with Sanskaar." All hostel students participate in daily Gayatri Yagya and Aarti. All students engage in Gayatri Mantra writing to promote concentration, spirituality, and discipline..

Admission Process

Follow the step-by-step process for admission to our institution. Ensure you have all
required documents ready for a smooth application experience.

Admission Steps

01
Collect Application Form (Offline/Online)

Obtain the application form either online through our portal or offline from the admission office.

02
Submit required documents

Attach all necessary documents with your completed application form and submit before the deadline.

03
Appear for counselling/merit verification

Attend the counselling session as per schedule. Your documents and eligibility will be verified.

04
Pay fees and confirm seat

Pay the required admission fees to confirm your seat in the program.

Documents Required

10th & 12th Marksheet
Transfer Certificate (TC)
Aadhar Card (Original And Photocopy)
Passport Size Photographs
Caste Certificate (If Applicable)
Income Certificate (If Applicable)
